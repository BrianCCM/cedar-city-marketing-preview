What We Do

Cedar City SEO, done completely.

Local SEO is not one thing. We handle every factor that determines where you rank in Cedar City search results.

Google Business Profile

Your GBP is the most valuable piece of local SEO real estate. We complete every field, add photos, build a posting cadence, and optimize for the exact Cedar City searches your customers use. We also set up a review system that keeps your profile improving month over month.

On-Page SEO

Title tags, meta descriptions, heading structure, internal links, and content depth. We make sure Google understands what your site is about and that every Cedar City keyword you should own is properly targeted on the right page.

Citation Building

Consistent business listings across Yelp, Apple Maps, Bing Places, and 50+ local directories tell Google your business is real and trustworthy. Inconsistent or missing citations are one of the most common reasons Cedar City businesses fail to rank in the map pack.

Local Content

Landing pages and content written for Cedar City specifically. Not generic copy with the city name swapped in, but pages that reflect the actual Cedar City market: the industries, the neighborhoods, and what people here actually search for.
