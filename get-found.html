Most Cedar City businesses start seeing measurable movement in Google rankings within 60 to 90 days. Full results (consistent top-3 placement in the map pack and organic results) typically take 4 to 6 months. The timeline depends on your starting point and how competitive your industry is locally.

Local SEO focuses specifically on ranking your business when people in Cedar City or Southern Utah search for your services. That means showing up in Google's map pack, local organic results, and Google Business Profile searches. Regular SEO targets broader, non-location-specific searches. For most local businesses, local SEO drives more relevant traffic: people who can actually walk through your door.

Three factors drive Google Maps rankings: a fully optimized Google Business Profile (complete info, photos, posts), consistent citations across directories like Yelp, Apple Maps, and local listings, and a steady stream of genuine customer reviews. We handle all three. Most businesses in Cedar City have significant gaps in at least one of these areas. That's usually where we start.
