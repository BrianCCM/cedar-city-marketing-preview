Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

Cedar City Marketing ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website cedarcitymarketing.com. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

We collect information in two ways:

  • Information you provide -- When you fill out a contact or inquiry form, we collect your name, business name, email address, and any details you include in your message. This is submitted through Formspree and delivered to our team.
  • Information collected automatically -- When you visit our site, analytics and advertising tools collect data about your visit, including pages viewed, time on site, general location, device type, and how you arrived at our site.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our site uses cookies and similar tracking technologies from the following services:

  • Google Analytics 4 (GA4) -- Helps us understand how visitors use our site. Data is aggregated and anonymous. Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Ads -- Used to show our ads to people who have previously visited our site (remarketing). Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Meta Pixel (Facebook/Instagram) -- Used to measure ad performance and show our ads to people who have visited our site on Facebook and Instagram. Meta's Privacy Policy.

These tools may set cookies on your device that persist across browsing sessions. We do not sell your personal data to any third party.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to inquiries submitted through our contact forms
  • To understand how visitors interact with our website and improve it
  • To show relevant ads to people who have visited our site

Your Choices

You can limit or opt out of tracking in the following ways:

  • Google Analytics -- Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Google Ads remarketing -- Manage preferences at Google Ad Settings
  • Meta ads -- Manage preferences in your Facebook Ad Preferences
  • Browser settings -- Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies through their settings

Data Retention

Form submissions are retained only as long as needed to respond to your inquiry. Analytics data is retained according to the default settings of each platform (GA4 retains event data for 14 months by default).

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
